Beef Croquette

Preparation time: 15minutes

Cooking time: 15minutes

Servings: 4 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1-Peel and chop the onions. Then cook the meat

2- add an egg, salt and pepper then add 400g/0,8lbs of mashed potatoes if you don't have potatoes use bread by crumbling it.

3- Then add the minced meat.

4-form small balls with the mixture. If you wish, you can roll them in the breadcrumbs to give them a crunch.

5- put the croquettes in an oven at 200°C/ 392°F for 15 to 20 minutes
