Potato Salad

Cooking time: 50minutes

Servings: 4 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1- Cook the potatoes in boiling water with a handful of salt

2- After 30 minutes of cooking, check the potatoes with a fork. If they are tender, take them out, if not, leave them for another 10 minutes.

3- When they are ready, peel them. tip: if you run them under cold water for a few minutes the skin will come off more easily

4-Finely chop the onions and add salt, pepper and the onions to the bowl. Then add a drizzle of oil on top and mix everything together.

5- Leave to cool for 1 hour in the fridge

6- When the mixture is cool, take them out and add a light drizzle of oil
