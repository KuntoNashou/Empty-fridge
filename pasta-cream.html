Pasta with cream and beef

Preparation time: 20minutes

Cooking time: 10minutes

Servings: 1 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1- Cook the pasta

2- Cut the onions in small cubes and cook them in a pan

3- When the onions are cooked add small pieces of steak and stir for 2 minutes

4- Add the pasta with the cream, season with salt and pepper and cook for a few minutes

5- All that's left to do is serve
