Empty Fridge

Empty Fridge is to make simple, fast, delicious and affordable food with the food in your fridge. By using it you also make an action for the planet.

Empty Fridge makes cooking easier and more simpler and more affordable for those just starting out.

Our app takes you step by step from the beginning to the end of the recipe.

Make your life easier by testing new recipes from around the world. You can try it for free and if you like it why not share it with your friends.

You noticed an error, a problem or a bug, or you have a suggestion to make? Do not hesitate to let us know. You can also contact us if you have good ideas for recipes.

Contact us with this email: emptyfridge@support.gg
