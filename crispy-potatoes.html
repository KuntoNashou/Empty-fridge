Crispy Potatoes

Preparation time: 5minutes

Cooking time: 20minutes

Servings: 2 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1- Heat your oven to 200°C / 392°F

2- Chop the potatoes into 1-inch chunks. Then in a bowl mix them with salt, pepper and oil.

3- Place and spread the seasoned potatoes on the baking sheet

4- Roast the potatoes for about 20 to 25 minutes

5- All that's left is to get them out
