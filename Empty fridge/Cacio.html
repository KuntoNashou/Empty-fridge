Cacio e pepe scrambled eggs

Preparation time: 5minutes

Cooking time: 10minutes

Servings: 1 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1- Break 2 eggs in a bowl and add a glass of milk. Then mix it all together

2.Cut your bread and cook it in a pan

3 - Cook your mixture in a pan with butter. While cooking, stir to make scrambled eggs.

4- Bake your bread with olive oil or butter

5- Once golden, put the bread on top and add the scrambled eggs. You can also add some grated cheese
