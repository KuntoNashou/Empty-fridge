2- In the meantime, cut the bread lengthwise to make a stick shape. To give it more taste if you wish you can add some butter and/or cheese y

3-As soon as the water boils, remove them from the pan. You can also leave them a little longer so that they don't sink.

4-All that remains is to break the top and dip your bread sticks in the egg
