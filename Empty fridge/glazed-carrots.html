Glazed Carrots

Cooking time: 40minutes

Servings: 3 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1- Peel and wash about 1kg/1lbs of carrots. Then cut them into slices

2- Put them in a saucepan and add a little butter or margarine with salt and a pinch of sugar. The carrots must be covered with water

3- Put the lid on and cook on low heat for about 30 to 40 minutes

4- Arrange the carrots in a shallow dish. tip: you can add some aromatic herbs to give it some taste
