Empty Fridge

Risoto

Cooking time: 25minutes

Servings: 2 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1- Cook 250g/0.55lbs of rice in a saucepan

2- Chop the onion. Heat 30g/0.06lbs of butter add the onion and rice. When everything is added, mix to make it shiny

3- Pour over the rice about 0.8L/2.1gallon. Add salt and pepper and cover. Let it rest on a very low heat for 17 minutes.

4-If the rice seems wet, leave it to cook for a few minutes longer, otherwise quickly add cheese or parmesan to the dish and serve.
