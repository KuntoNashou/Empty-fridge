Macaroni and Cheese

Preparation time: 10minutes

Cooking time: 15minutes

Servings: 4 People

All ingredients that you need!

Preparation Step

1-Boil salted water and put in a box of macaroni

2- While pasta is cooking, place in a saucepan over low heat: 1/4 cup butter, salt and pepper, 1/4 cup flour and stir for a few minutes.

3- Then lightly pour 2 cups of milk while stirring. Then let cook for 5 minutes. Finally add 2 to 3 cups of cheese and stir it until it is melted.

4-When the macaroni is cooked, take it out of the water, drain it and add it to the sauce, stir it a little and serve it
